Jupiter Retail

Jupiter Retail sells products exclusively on Amazon.com and needed a site to show to manufacturers and customers to serve as a point of contact and to give basic information.

Next Level Web Studio have partnered with Jupiter Retail to provide all of its web and graphic design needs, we will also be providing ongoing support for future websites for brands created by Jupiter Retail that will follow the design cues set by the parent company.

HireNet Ltd

HireNet is a plant hire company that required a site that showed all of the different equipment that is available from them. The client wanted a professional site that had easy access to the information about the products and services.

The client also wanted a consistent look across all pages and a consistent look to all the products shown including incorporating the company logo into the products. And also incorporate a strong colour theme while maintaining usability on both the desktop and mobile.

HireNet was created in conjunction with Doive Designs
